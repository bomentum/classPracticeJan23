Kaffir Curry

Ingredients

3-4 kaffir lime leaves

  • Protein of Choice
  • Thai Chili Peppers
  • Veggies of Choice
  • 2-3 slices of ginger
  • 2-3 tbl of soy sauce
  • 1 tbl of fish sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves smashed
  • Sliced Onions or Onion Pearls
  • Veggie Seasoning salt or Lawry's salt

Saute ginger, garlic, and lime leaves in a little bit of oil. Add protein and soy sauce, fish sauce, and salt and stir to get seasoning mixed in well. Lower temp and cover till protein is cooked. Add veggies and onions and stir. Turn off heat and cover for few minutes for softer veggies or leave on heat for a few more minutes for hardier veggies.
